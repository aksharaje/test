# Zendesk Connector

## Overview
The Zendesk Connector allows Product Managers to seamlessly integrate customer support data from Zendesk into their product management workflow. By using this tool, PMs can gain valuable insights from customer interactions to make informed product decisions.

## Key Capabilities
- Connect Zendesk customer support data to your product management dashboard
- Analyze customer feedback and support tickets for product improvement insights
- Track customer satisfaction trends for better product prioritization

## How to Use
To start using the Zendesk Connector, begin by entering your Zendesk account credentials. Then, select the specific data you want to import into your product management tool. Once connected, you can start analyzing customer support data alongside your product development process.

## Configuration & Fields
- **Return URL**: The URL where you want to redirect users after authentication.
  - **What it's for**: Specifies the destination URL after completing the authentication process.
  - **Example**: "https://www.productmanagementapp.com/dashboard"

By simplifying the integration of Zendesk data into your product management workflow, the Zendesk Connector empowers Product Managers to make data-driven decisions that align with customer needs and feedback.
