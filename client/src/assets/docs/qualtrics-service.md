# Qualtrics Connector

## Overview
The Qualtrics Connector allows Product Managers to seamlessly integrate Qualtrics survey data into their product planning process. By utilizing this tool, PMs can gather valuable insights from customer feedback surveys to inform strategic decision-making.

## Key Capabilities
- Connect Qualtrics survey data
- Analyze customer feedback
- Track survey results

## How to Use
To leverage the Qualtrics Connector, start by entering the URL of the Qualtrics survey you want to connect. The tool will then retrieve the survey data and present it in a user-friendly format for analysis. You can track changes in customer feedback over time and gain actionable insights to enhance your product strategy.

## Configuration & Fields
- **Field Name**: Return URL
  - **Purpose**: Specify the URL to redirect users after connecting to Qualtrics.
  - **Example**: "https://www.yourwebsite.com/thankyou"

Remember to follow the provided guidelines for a seamless experience with the Qualtrics Connector.
