
# Story Generator

## Overview
The Story Generator tool helps Product Managers create compelling narratives for their products, enhancing communication with stakeholders and customers. By generating structured storylines, PMs can effectively convey product vision, features, and benefits.

## Key Capabilities
- Create engaging storylines
- Analyze narrative structure
- Track story evolution

## How to Use
Start by outlining the key elements of your product story, such as the problem statement, solution, and value proposition. Use the tool to generate a cohesive narrative that resonates with your audience. Refine the story based on feedback and iterate to create a compelling product narrative.

## Configuration & Fields
- **Connected Knowledge Base**
  - **Purpose**: Select the knowledge base to draw inspiration and information from for your story.
  - **Example**: Industry reports, customer testimonials

- **Owner**
  - **Purpose**: Identify the owner or primary user associated with the story.
  - **Example**: Product Manager, Marketing Lead

- **Data**
  - **Purpose**: Provide the data necessary to generate the story.
  - **Example**: Product features, customer feedback

- **Narrative Type**
  - **Purpose**: Specify the type of narrative you want to create.
  - **Example**: Success story, use case scenario
