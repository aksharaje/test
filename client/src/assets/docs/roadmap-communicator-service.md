# Roadmap Communicator

## Overview
Roadmap Communicator helps Product Managers effectively manage and communicate roadmap and scenario information with stakeholders. It enables PMs to create, organize, and present sessions and presentations in a structured manner, fostering collaboration and alignment across teams.

## Key Capabilities
- Create new communicator sessions for roadmaps or scenarios
- Retrieve and manage existing sessions and presentations
- Delete sessions and associated presentations
- Access available audience types and configurations

## How to Use
To begin, start by creating a new communicator session based on your roadmap or scenario. Add presentations to the session to convey key information effectively. You can then share the session with stakeholders for feedback and collaboration. Finally, manage and organize sessions as needed, ensuring all relevant information is up-to-date and accessible.

## Configuration & Fields
- **CommunicatorSessionCreate**: 
  - **Purpose**: Data to create a new communicator session.
  - **Example**: Details of the roadmap or scenario for the session.

- **User**: 
  - **Purpose**: Identifies the owner or user associated with the session.
  - **Example**: John Doe, Product Manager.

- **Roadmap Session Reference**: 
  - **Purpose**: Identifies the specific roadmap for which communicator sessions are retrieved.
  - **Example**: Q3 Product Roadmap.

- **Presentation ID**: 
  - **Purpose**: Identifies a specific presentation within a session.
  - **Example**: Presentation #001.

- **Audience Types**: 
  - **Purpose**: Provides available audience types and their configurations.
  - **Example**: Internal stakeholders, External partners.
