# Settings Manager

## Overview
The Settings Manager helps you customize and manage key configurations for your product or project, ensuring that your settings are tailored to your specific needs and objectives.

## Key Capabilities
- Set custom configurations
- Retrieve specific settings

## How to Use
To start managing your settings, begin by setting key configurations that align with your project goals. You can then easily retrieve these settings whenever needed to ensure consistency and efficiency in your workflow.

## Configuration & Fields
- **Session**: Represents the work session you are currently working on.
  - **What it's for**: Identifies the context in which the settings are being applied.
  - **Example**: "Marketing Campaign Q3 2022"
  
- **Key**: Refers to the unique identifier for a specific setting.
  - **What it's for**: Helps distinguish between different settings within your configurations.
  - **Example**: "EmailNotificationFrequency"
  
- **Value**: Represents the actual value assigned to a specific setting.
  - **What it's for**: Defines the specific configuration or option for a setting.
  - **Example**: "Daily"
  
- **Description**: Provides additional information or context about a setting.
  - **What it's for**: Offers a brief description to clarify the purpose or usage of a setting.
  - **Example**: "Controls the frequency of email notifications sent to users"
